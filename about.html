OUR STORY

j15 five is an interior design company founded in 2021. Our founder started with her first home, from which the desire to pursue further led to paying clients. J15 focuses on creating warm, inviting and exquisite interior spaces that are also practical for everyday living.

J15five stands for John 15 verse 5, referring to the bearing of good fruit when one abides to the Lord Jesus. A testament to the core of our values as a company, Love, Respect, and Excellence. For our valuable clients, each project is a labour of love, respect for our clients and and excellent execution
