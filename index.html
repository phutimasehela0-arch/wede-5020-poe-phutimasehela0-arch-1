Our Services

How we elevate your space

PHASE 1( Concept Development )

Space planning, concept development, preliminary concepts for finishes, present suggestions for products/fixtures/fittings. Full amount Paid before Presenting to Client (Client can choose to implement concept without Designer) Differs for Commercial projects. Fixed rate applies (R/space) .

PHASE 2 ( Preparation of detailed documentation )

after the initial concept is signed off, Contract signed between J15five and client to move forward. Finalisation of the interior selections and preparing scaled design drawings and documentation including lighting/electrical plans (if required, at extra cost). Full amount Paid at the end of the project. Priced at a fixed fee for design work plus an hourly rate for meetings and site visits

Phase 3 ( Construction period )

Includes the purchasing of the PC items, visiting suppliers and the undertaking regular site visits to monitor design intent and progress. Invoice to be sent to client at the end of project. Hourly rate applies

Phase 4 ( Furnishing and Styling )

Developing a concept for furniture, soft furnishings and styling. Normally done in conjunction with Phases 1 and 2, but the finalization of furniture and accessories sourcing and purchasing is done in this phase. To be Paid after project is done, fixed rate applies (R/space)
